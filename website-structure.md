## Problem Section

**Label:** The problem

**Headline:** Sim and real shouldn't feel like different worlds.

**Points:**

1. **Fragmented toolchains** : Isaac Sim, MuJoCo, ROS 2, custom data pipelines. Each with its own config format and zero shared state.

2. **Manual sim-to-real transfer** : Teams spend weeks triaging whether a failure was a sim gap or a policy failure. No structured transfer workflow exists.

3. **No closed loop** : Real-world failures have no path back into simulation. Every failure is diagnosed in isolation and never improves the sim.

---

## Platform Section

**Label:** The platform

**Headline:** One workspace. The entire development loop.

**Subheadline:** Orex connects every phase of robot development : simulation, transfer, deployment, and feedback : into a single, continuous system.

**Features:**

1. **Simulation Hub** : Unified interface for Isaac Sim and MuJoCo. Configure environments, launch training runs, and manage assets without reformatting configs.

2. **Sim-to-Real Pipeline** : Structured transfer workflows with domain randomization configs, validation checkpoints, and side-by-side comparison tools.

3. **Real-to-Sim Feedback** : Capture real-world failure modes and automatically surface the sim conditions needed to reproduce and fix them.

4. **Fleet & Deployment** : Connect to physical robots anywhere. Push OTA policy updates, monitor live telemetry, roll back broken deployments.

5. **Data & Observability** : Multimodal data capture across sim runs and real deployments. Search every log. Curate datasets for your next training run.

6. **Compliance** : Built-in compliance tracking for teams shipping into regulated environments. Not a checklist at the end : woven into the workflow.

## How It Works Section

**Label:** The loop

**Headline:** Build in sim. Ship to real. Learn. Repeat.

**Subheadline:** Orex owns the canonical robotics development loop : every phase connected, no manual handoffs.

**Steps:**

1. **Build in sim** : Isaac Sim & MuJoCo in one workspace
2. **Validate** : Transfer checkpoints & domain randomization
3. **Deploy to real** : OTA updates to physical fleet
4. **Learn from real** : Failures flow back into sim automatically
